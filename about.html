About

Steven P. Millard, Ph.D., is an independent statistical consultant and also Senior Biostatistician at the VA Puget Sound Health Care System in Seattle, Washington,and has worked in the field of environmental and health care statistics for over 25 years. He has worked at the US Geological Survey, the University of Washington, CH2M Hill, the University of California at Santa Barbara, Saint Martin’s College, Insightful Corporation, and the Cystic Fibrosis Therapeutics Development Network Coordinating Center. In 1990 he developed the training program in S-PLUS while at Statistical Sciences (the creator of S-PLUS), and later developed the S-PLUS module EnvironmentalStats for S-PLUS. His newest software project is EnvStats, an R package for environmental statistics. Dr. Millard has taught numerous courses in statistics and software to professionals in the United States and Europe, including at the US EPA, Merck, and the National Security Agency. Past consulting projects include drinking water contaminants analysis for the US Geological Survey; expert witness for health care and environmental litigation; analyses for biomedical firms; climate modeling for U.S. Army Corps of Engineers; and software for automated home appraisal (sold to TransUnion). Dr. Millard is the co-author of textbooks on environmental statistics and statistics for drug development. He holds a B.A. in Mathematics from Pomona College, and an M.S. and Ph.D. in Biostatistics from the University of Washington.
